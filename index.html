Welcome to my studio!
My name is Mia and I have been studying piano since the age of four, am a junior at Henry M. Jackson High School, and continue to further my studies under Nino Merabishvili. I have won awards at various music festivals, and most recently, first prize at Eastside Scholarship Competition and a silver at the Pacific International Piano Competition and Kings Peak Music Competition. I also have participated and earned awards at competitions for my own compositions, including the International Piano Composition Contest, Guild.
Mission
Having studied the piano for so long, I am eager to pass on the knowledge I have obtained to young, beginner piano students. Music has played a huge role in my life, instilling values such as dedication and passion within me. My goal is to help students find joy and entertainment through music, while consistently improving their skills.
Who should join?
Lessons are offered to beginner students, starting from age 4. If you have a child who has never touched a piano but expresses interest in music, or they fool around on the piano and are interested in building a solid foundation, don't hesitate to contact me! Basic theory, ear training and sight reading are also taught to fuel musical development. A consultation through Zoom will be conducted, as well as a trial lesson, for free, before moving on with lessons.
