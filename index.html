Welcome to my studio!


My name is Mia and I have been studying piano since the age of four, am a rising junior at Henry M. Jackson High School, and continue to further my studies under Nino Merabishvili. I have won awards at various music festivals, and most recently, first prize at Eastside Scholarship Competition and a silver at the Pacific International Piano Competition. I also have participated and earned awards at competitions for my own compositions, including the International Piano Composition Contest, Guild.

Mission


Having studied the piano for so long, I am eager to pass on the knowledge I have obtained to young, beginner piano students. Music has played a huge role in my life, instilling values such as dedication and passion within me. My goal is to help students find joy and entertainment through music, while consistently improving their skills.

Who should join?

I currently offer lessons to beginner students, starting from age 4. Basic theory, ear training and sight reading are also taught to fuel musical development. A consultation through Zoom will be conducted, for free, before moving on with lessons.
